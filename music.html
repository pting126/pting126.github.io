Context & Community Impact

As a core member of Seagull K for over 10 years, I have performed in 100+ concerts and festivals. The group focuses on cultural storytelling and linguistic preservation through multilingual repertoire (Mandarin, Hakka, Taiwanese, Indigenous).

Key Outreach: Founded the HCAC (Hsinchu Contemporary A Cappella Center) and "Project K" youth training program to mentor the next generation of vocalists.
