Context & Community Impact

As a long-time member of Seagull K, I have performed in more than 100 concerts and festivals across Taiwan and abroad. Our repertoire spans Mandarin, Hakka, Taiwanese, and Indigenous languages, with arrangements that emphasize blend, groove, and storytelling.

These ensemble experiences shaped how I think about dataset design (e.g., ACappellaSet), source separation for SATB voices, and rehearsal tools that visualize timing, tuning, and dynamics in meaningful ways.

Outreach: co-founding HCAC (Hsinchu Contemporary A Cappella Center) and mentoring youth ensembles through programs such as "Project K," connecting community work with research on accessible AI tools.

Individual Works

Composition & Production

NTHU Graduation Song

Full Band Production • 2024

Composer Producer Vocalist

As the main arranger and producer, I led the 2024 National Tsing Hua University graduation anthem, coordinating band tracking, vocal sessions, and the overall sonic direction for the event.

The project showcases my ability to manage large recording sessions, blend live instruments with layered vocals, and build an emotional arc that matches a milestone graduation ceremony.

A piano-vocal ballad that focuses on lyric-driven storytelling and intimate performance. All instrumentation, recording, and rough mixing were done by me.
