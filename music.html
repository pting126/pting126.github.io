Context & Community Impact

As a core member of Seagull K for over a decade, I have performed in more than 100 concerts and festivals across Taiwan and abroad. Our repertoire spans Mandarin, Hakka, Taiwanese, and Indigenous languages, emphasizing cultural storytelling and vocal blend.

These experiences directly inform my research on AI tools that respect vocal nuance, phrasing, and the realities of ensemble rehearsal, especially in small-group contemporary a cappella settings.

Key outreach: co-founding HCAC (Hsinchu Contemporary A Cappella Center) and mentoring youth ensembles through "Project K" and other educational initiatives.
